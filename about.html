Who We Are

Rad Motel is a modern and affordable accommodation facility located in Kakiika along Kyamugorani Road in Mbarara. We provide a safe and comfortable environment for travelers, families, and business guests.

Our goal is to offer quality hospitality services at reasonable prices while maintaining cleanliness, security, and excellent customer care.
